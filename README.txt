
BTC EXCHANGE is an application that checks the currency market at every configurable period of time to retrieve the current exchange rate of bitcoin (BTC) to the US dollar (USD).

The period set is 10 minutes (600000 ms)

To view the latest exchange rate go to : http://localhost:7000/v1/exchange-rate/latest

To view the exchange rate over a period of time go to: http://localhost:7000/v1/exchange-rate/list?startDate=2021/01/01&endDate=2021/12/12
Where startDate and endDate are date parameters.
